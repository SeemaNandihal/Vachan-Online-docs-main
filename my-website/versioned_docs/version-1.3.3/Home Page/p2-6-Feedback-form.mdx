A **feedback form** is offered in order to maintain a constant communication with Vachan online users and to understand better the overall user experience.

You are able to report a problem or submit a query about Vachan Online by completing the feedback form.

To ensure that all of our visitors have a pleasant browsing experience, we keep track of any issues or queries and make ongoing website improvements.

You can also email us at thevachanproject@bridgeconn.com with your concerns and suggestions.
All feedbacks are appreciated!
